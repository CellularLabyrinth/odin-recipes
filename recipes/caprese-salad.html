Caprese Salad

Description

Caprese salad is a classic light dish from Italy, a simple pairing of mozzarella cheese with thick tomato slices, all drizzled over with a lovely vinegar reduction and sprinkled with basil. The result is a fresh, satisfying salad that naturally suits the summer climate, but whose elegance cannot be denied at any time of year.

Ingredients

  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Large tomato
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Honey
  • Basil leaves

Steps

  1. Slice the mozzarella and the tomato into flat, regular pieces around 1-2cm thick
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the balsamic vinegar with the honey
  3. Lay the tomato and mozzarella slices onto a plate, one on top of the other
  4. Sprinkle the basil leaves over the slices, then drizzle over the reduction
